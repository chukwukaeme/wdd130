Our History

Founded in 2005, RaftXperience started with just two rafts and a dream — to share the thrill of whitewater rafting with adventurers from all walks of life. Over the years, we’ve grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies in the region, serving thousands of happy rafters each season.

From small family floats to Class V rapid expeditions, our passion for the river and commitment to safety have remained our top priorities. Join us as we continue to create unforgettable experiences, one wave at a time.
